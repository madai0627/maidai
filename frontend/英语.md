### 图表作文模板
#### 模板一
The chart gives us an overall picture of the __________(图表主题). The first thing we notice is that_______________(图表最大特点). This means that __________,(进一步说明).
We can see from the statistics given that ___________(图表细节一). After v-ing_________(细节一中的第一个变化), the ____ (紧跟着的变化:幅度+时间). The figures also tells us that______________________(图表细节二). (数据位置，如In the second column), we can see that ____________accounts for _______(进一步描述).
Judging from these figures, we can draw the conclusion that___________(结论). The reason for this, as far as I am concerned is that_____________(给出原因). / It is high time that we should _________ (发出倡议)

#### 模板二
1. 开头段
As can be seen from the (图表类型，如 “bar chart” “pie chart”), it clearly shows (图表总体反映的内容，如 “the changes of people's consumption patterns in recent years”).
2. 中间段
Describe the details of the chart. For instance, (描述第一个数据或趋势). Meanwhile, (描述第二个相关数据或趋势). Noticeably, (突出某个显著变化或特点).
3. 结尾段
Based on the above analysis, we can conclude that (总结图表反映的核心内容). And it is predictable that (对未来趋势的简单预测).

### 议论文模板
#### 论证观点类
1. 开头段（提出观点）
（主题句）_________(通过背景描写引出论点或直接提出论点)。
（扩展句一）_________(阐述论点中蕴涵的道理或正确性表现)。
（扩展句二）Besides/ In addition, _________(进一步阐述论点中的道理或正确性表现)。/ The truth of it is deep and profound. / The truth of it is self-evident.
2. 中间段（列举论据）
（主题句）There are numerous examples supporting this argument。/ Many remarkable examples contribute to this argument.
（扩展句一）A case in point is _________(第一个例子)。
（扩展句二）_________(进一步说明例一)。
（扩展句三）For another example, _________(第二个例子)。
（扩展句四）_________(进一步说明例二)。
3. 结尾段（总结观点）
（主题句）All the above-mentioned tell us that/ It is no doubt that/ Judging from the evidence offered, we might safely draw the conclusion that _________(重申观点)。
（扩展句）_________(提出建议或指出注意事项)。
（结尾句）To conclude/ Therefore, _________(总结全文，呼应论点)。

#### 观点对比类
1. 开头段
With the rapid development of technology, _________(引出相关现象，如online learning) has become a trend. Some argue that _________(一方观点，如it has advantages of flexibility and cost-effectiveness), while others believe _________(另一方观点，如it lacks face-to-face interaction). From my perspective, _________(明确立场，如both have pros and cons).
2. 中间段
On the one hand, _________(支持方观点展开说明). For instance, _________(举例，如students in remote areas can access courses easily through online learning). On the other hand, _________(反对方观点展开说明). A survey shows that _________(数据，如over 60% of teachers feel online classes reduce student engagement).
3. 结尾段
In conclusion, _________(折中结论，如a hybrid model combining online and offline learning is ideal). By integrating _________(线上优势) with _________(线下优势), we can maximize _________(相关领域效益，如educational efficiency).
